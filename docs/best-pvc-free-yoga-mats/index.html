Quick picks by use case

Hot yoga / sweat: prioritize wet grip (often rubber-based) and accept that “rubber smell” may exist at first.

Sensitive nose / low odor: prioritize transparent materials + good returns; avoid vague “proprietary foam.”

Travel: lighter mats trade cushioning for portability. Consider mat+towel setups.

FAQ

Is PVC-free the same as non-toxic? No. It removes one common plastic, but additives and coatings still matter.

What’s the safest material? There’s no universal “safest.” We focus on transparency, certifications, and practical performance.
