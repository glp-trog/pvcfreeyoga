How we rank

We’re optimizing for one thing: a mat that’s PVC-free with clear materials disclosure. Everything else—grip, odor, durability—comes after the materials story makes sense.

Rubric (v1)

Category What we look for Red flags
Materials transparency Exact composition and coatings; clear “PVC-free” statement “Eco foam”, “proprietary blend”, no composition details
Grip Dry + wet grip, especially for sweat/hot yoga Only vague claims (“ultra grip”) with no context
Odor Realistic odor guidance; return policy if smell is intolerable Overpromises (“zero smell”) without caveats
Durability Thickness, wear, peeling, flaking; long-term reviews Reports of cracking, sticky residue, delamination
Care & cleaning Clear instructions; what to avoid; washable surfaces No guidance, or advice that conflicts with material reality
Policies Warranty, returns, shipping; customer support Hard-to-find policies; restrictive returns

Editorial note: we use affiliate links when available. That does not override the rubric. If a mat has unclear composition, we don’t recommend it.
