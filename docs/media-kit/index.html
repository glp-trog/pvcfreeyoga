Media kit

PVCFreeYoga is a mats-first guide to PVC-free yoga mats. We focus on materials transparency and practical performance (grip, odor, durability, care).

Positioning

One-liner: PVC-free yoga mats, without the marketing fog.

What we do: comparison guides + materials explainers + problem pages (odor, sweaty grip, care).

What we avoid: absolute claims (“toxin-free”). We use concrete, checkable language (PVC-free, composition disclosure, policies).

Editorial policy

  • Materials-first: composition and coatings matter more than aesthetics.
  • Evidence > vibes: we prefer explicit composition, certifications (when relevant), and clear warranty/returns.
  • Affiliate disclosure: some outbound links may earn a commission at no cost to the reader.
